Research Librarian Materials:

This file contains the prompts used in the Research Librarian Study.  

Note that in the follow-up prompts, x = the number of papers given in the GPT’s first response; y = 20 – x.  Also, note that this follow-up prompt was repeated, using the same formula (x = number of papers generated in previous responses; y = 20 – x) until ChatGPT either generated 20 references or clearly acknowledged that it was unable to do so.

Broad Categories:

Implicit Association Test:

Initial Prompt:

Good morning – I’m building a repository of peer-reviewed scientific papers for a research project I'm working on. Specifically, I'm exploring the Implicit Association Test. I'd like you to find 20 of the most important and influential peer-reviewed papers about the Implicit Association Test. For each, please include a citation and a very brief summary. Please include ONLY papers specifically related to the Implicit Association Test.

Follow-up Prompt:

Thank you, that gives me a good start but it is only x papers. Please continue by finding an additional y of the most important and influential peer-reviewed papers about the Implicit Association Test. For each, please include a citation and a very brief summary. Please include ONLY papers specifically related to the Implicit Association Test. Please do not include any of the x papers you just mentioned.
